Dance Portfolio

I love to dance. I've been dancing since I was two and a half years old. All of the hours spent dancing has been an incredibly journey of self-refinement, hard-work, passion, teamwork, and friendship. I am very blessed to have been able to dance with the people I have and I am continually inspired by them every time I get the privilege of watching a performance. I am currently a member of Groove Nation, an urban dance crew at my college. My first semester of college, I was part of the 5C Dance Company and I choreographed two dances for the semester-end showcase. Previously, I had been doing competitive dance in the styles of tap, hip-hop, jazz, contemporary, musical theater, and lyrical.
